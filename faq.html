FAQs

What time should I arrive?

Guests are encouraged to arrive at least 20 minutes before the start of the ceremony. For members of the entourage, please be present by 2:00 PM for the getting-ready photo shoot.

Is there a dress code?

The dress code for our ceremony is strictly formal: elegant evening gowns or cocktail dresses for the ladies, dark formal suits for the gentlemen.

Is parking available?

Yes, there will be ample parking slots available at the ceremony and reception venues. A valet service is also available for a minimal charge.
