Travel & Accommodation

Here are some details to help you plan your trip:

  • Venue Address: Green Acres Farm, 123 Country Lane, Hometown.
  • Directions: ~30 min drive from Hometown Airport. We will provide a shuttle from the Central Station at 13:00 on the day.
  • Parking: Available on-site at the farm (free of charge).
  • Nearby Hotels: Hometown Inn (5 km away), Lakeside B&B (8 km), Grand Hotel (City Center).
  • Camping: For the adventurous, there's space to pitch a tent at the farm (please let us know in advance).

If you need any help with travel or accommodations, feel free to reach out to us.
